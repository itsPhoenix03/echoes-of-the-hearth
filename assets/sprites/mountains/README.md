# Biome mountain silhouettes

Large deterministic landmark/blocker assets. Use them in sparse mountain chains, not as one sprite on every elevated tile.

- `mountain_woods.svg`: Woods ridges.
- `mountain_dunes.svg`: Dunes mesas and dry ranges.
- `mountain_spire.svg`: Frozen Spire peaks.
- `mountain_marsh.svg`: low mossy crags around Marsh high ground.
- `mountain_core_obsidian.svg`: Core/volcanic obsidian ridges.

Mountain collision and impassable footprints must be represented in shared/server world data; the SVG alone is visual.
