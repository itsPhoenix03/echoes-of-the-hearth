Survival: eat (hunt + cook at campfire) and drink (E at water, click 💧) or you'll starve. Campfires heal you, set your respawn, and shield wooden buildings from night Blight Storms. Deserts need a Heat Cloak, snow needs a Fur Cloak. Shelter beside structures in sandstorms; reach a campfire in blizzards.

Building: open the bag, click a 🔨 item, then click a tile. Click an existing wall/shelter to stack it higher. Walls block monsters; brutes smash them. Place farm plots outdoors to grow wheat and glowcap. Decor items (banners, rugs, vases, fences) are zone-restricted — your Bag shows a hint when they cannot be placed. Mines can be furnished as underground hideouts: place chests and beds inside dug tunnels.

Boats: craft one, open your Bag, click the boat to SELECT it, then walk into the sea to sail. Without a selected boat you will swim — much slower, and hot or freezing seas deal damage over time. Icebergs destroy wooden boats — a Starmetal-reinforced hull smashes through.

Goal: follow the hidden notes 📜 across five distant islands, awaken all 4 Monoliths with forged Cores, build the World Engine at the Core Void, survive the final assault.
